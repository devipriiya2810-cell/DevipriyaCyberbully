What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, data, and programs from digital attacks, damage, or unauthorized access.These attacks are often aimed at accessing,changing, or destroying sensitive information.Everyone who uses the internet ,students,workers,or business must now how to stay safe online.

Why Cybersecurity and Safe Internet Practices is important?

Cybersecurity and safe internet practices are important because they protect personal and organizational data from theft and misuse, prevent financial loss and business disruption, and safeguard against identity theft and fraud.

Tips for Staying Safe Online

  • Use strong and unique passwords for each account.
  • avoid click unknown link in emails.
  • Install antivirus and keep your system updated.
  • Do not share personal details like passwords or bank info online.
